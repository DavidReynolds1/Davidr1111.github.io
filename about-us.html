Elementary Programs

Students in grades 5-7 will explore the exciting world of basic programming and circuitry using small yet powerful physical computing devices such as Micro:bit and Circuit Playground.

Our programs are led by experienced staff, the workshops are free and run from 9:30 am - 12 noon with a pizza lunch. Three sessions are held at the UNH Durham campus; two are at the UNH Manchester campus - each session has different activities.

Some experience with Scratch, Code.org or LEGO Mindstorm is helpful, but not necessary.
